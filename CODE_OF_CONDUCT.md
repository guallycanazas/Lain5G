# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

Project participants are expected to communicate respectfully, welcome
constructive technical disagreement, give actionable feedback, respect privacy
and safety boundaries, and acknowledge the work of others.

Harassment, discrimination, threats, sexualized attention, deliberate
intimidation, doxxing, publication of secrets or personal data, and sustained
disruptive behavior are unacceptable.

## Scope

This policy applies in repository discussions, issues, reviews, project-linked
communication, and public representation of the project. Technical claims must
be evidence-based, and safety or disclosure concerns must not be dismissed to
accelerate a release.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit or remove inappropriate material, request a change in
behavior, restrict participation, or ban repeated or severe violations. Actions
should be proportionate, documented privately when appropriate, and avoid
unnecessary disclosure of reporters' identities.
